CONCLUSION
The collected data offers insights into students' club preferences, success rates, and the relationship between grade levels and club choices. Some clubs are consistently popular, while others have varying acceptance rates. Age, grade level, and batch influence students' club preferences. Limitations include missing data from certain grade levels, hindering a comprehensive understanding of overall club preferences. Nonetheless, the dataset provides a basis for future research and offers valuable insights to school administration for optimizing club offerings.
