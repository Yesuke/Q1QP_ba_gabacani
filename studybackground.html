STUDY BACKGROUND
The project "Alternative Learning Activities: A Pisay Scholar's Pick" aimed to collect information on the most appealing Alternative Learning Activities (ALAs) among Pisay Scholars at their campus. The data set includes 80 students from various sections and grade levels, serving as a sample from the larger population of 480 students eligible for ALAs in grades 7, 8, 9, and 10. The data was collected through an online survey. The data set comprises four variables, including two qualitative ones: Preferred ALA and State of Acceptance within the preferred ALA, and two quantitative ones: age and the number of ALAs of interest. Additional variables such as name, section, and grade level were included for formality but provided little relevant information for the project.
